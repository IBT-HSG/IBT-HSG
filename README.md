The Institute of Behavioral Science & Technology at the University of St. Gallen (IBT-HSG) is a new, interdisciplinary research institute that conducts world-class research to understand, predict, and improve the human-technology relationship. We study behavior and technology from a human-centered perspective. We use latest methods to conduct fundamental research and to study technological applications as well as their implications for individuals, organizations, and society at large.
